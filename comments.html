Leave a Comment

For a long time I refrained from adding a guestbook or some other sort of commenting system on this site, as I saw it as unnecessary, but my view on the matter has lately changed: I now see it as a good way to promote discussion and, ultimately, free speech.

I chose HTML Comment Box to deliver this functionality, as it is free of charge, lightweight, straightforward (i.e. no CAPTCHA or other verification), and permits anonymous comments. I encourage everyone to leave a comment, positive or negative; I treasure honesty far more than fake niceness, and I will not delete comments.
