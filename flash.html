Flash Animations

On occasion I have made my own original Flash animations, partly for fun, and partly to honor Adobe Flash, which will never die. The style is inspired somewhat by YouTube Poops, and, this being a 2000s nostalgia site, you should expect many references to things from that decade. I will be distributing these only as SWF files (rather than as video files) in order to encourage you to download Flash if you haven't already; the preferred way to view them is embedded in the page. If you enjoy them, please feel free to copy and spread them far and wide across the Internet.

These animations, like everything else on the Internet, should not be taken seriously.

  • Are You a Wizard?: For some reason, Alex Russo loves to talk about wizards.
  • Based and Cringepilled: What does moot really think of us?
  • Cory, Cory, Cory: Cory Baxter and Newton Livingston III for President and Vice President 2020!
  • iCarly Makes Hannah Montana Cry: Miley was never quite the same afterwards.
  • Moar!: A small tribute to Kortez3000.
  • Sunshine 2021: Because referencing obscure 2006 Newgrounds fads makes me cool!
  • Take It Easy, Adolf!: How to prevent a world war.
  • The Marine Life: Spengbab has always been with me, in my heart.
  • The New and Improved George Lopez Opening: Featuring Vic Laser Eyes Palmero and Ernie The Sun Cardenas!
  • Where's My Birdhouse?: Kevin Jonas is quite fond of birdhouses. (Source: Camp Rock.)
